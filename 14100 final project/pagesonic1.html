Sonic The Hedgehog

Story:

The world is introduced to a blue blur of a hero, Sonic, who is determined to thwart the nefarious plans of Dr. Robotnik, who aims to collect the mystical Chaos Emeralds to fuel his malevolent machines and establish his twisted vision of order. Sonic races through various zones, overcoming obstacles and enemies, to prevent Dr. Robotnik from capturing innocent animals and turning them into robotic minions. It's a high-speed adventure where good battles evil, and freedom fights against control.
