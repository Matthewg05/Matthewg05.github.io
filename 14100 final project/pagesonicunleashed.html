Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Unleashed

Story:

Sonic the Hedgehog, embarks on a quest to repair a world fractured by the nefarious Dr. Eggman. Using a powerful laser, Eggman shatters the planet, unleashing the malevolent entity known as Dark Gaia. This catastrophic event not only splits the continents but also transforms Sonic into a Werehog at night.
