Sonic Generations

Story:

Sonic the Hedgehog celebrates his birthday with his friends when suddenly, a mysterious force creates time holes, pulling everyone back into different times and places. As Sonic traverses these time-warped zones, he encounters both his classic self and Dr. Robotnik, who is intent on harnessing the power of the Time Eater to erase Sonic's victories from history.
