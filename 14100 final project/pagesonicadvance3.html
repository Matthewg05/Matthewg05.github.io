Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Advance 3

Story:

Dr. Eggman using a device to split the world into seven separate regions, each with its own unique environment and theme. Sonic and his allies, including Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Cream, and a new character named Gemerl, must travel through these regions, defeat enemies, and collect Chaos Emeralds to restore the world to its original state.
