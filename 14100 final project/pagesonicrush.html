Sonic Rush

Story:

Sonic the Hedgehog discovers a parallel dimension called the Sol Dimension, where he encounters Blaze the Cat, a princess with pyrokinetic abilities. Sonic learns that Blaze is on a mission to protect the Sol Emeralds, which are counterparts to the Chaos Emeralds in Sonic's dimension. Meanwhile, Dr. Eggman and his doppleganger, Eggman Nega, plot to steal the Sol Emeralds for their own nefarious purposes. They manipulate the two dimensions, causing them to merge and creating chaos in both worlds. Sonic and Blaze team up to stop Dr. Eggman and Eggman Nega, embarking on a journey through various zones in both dimensions. Along the way, they encounter familiar enemies, including Eggman's robotic creations
