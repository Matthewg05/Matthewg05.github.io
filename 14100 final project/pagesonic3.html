Sonic 3 and Knuckles

Story:

Sonic and his steadfast companion, Miles Prower, better known as Tails, embark on a quest to thwart the nefarious Dr. Robotnik. Dr. Robotnik, in his insatiable quest for power, seeks to repair his Death Egg warship, using the energy of the mythical Floating Island's Master Emerald to fuel its engines. The island, guarded by Knuckles the Echidna, who has been deceived by Dr. Robotnik into thinking Sonic and Tails are the villains, becomes the battleground for an epic confrontation.
