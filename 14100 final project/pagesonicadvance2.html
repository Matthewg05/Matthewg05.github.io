Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Advance 2

Story:

Dr. Eggman, once again, decides to conquer the world. This time, he creates a massive space station called the Egg Utopia and plans to use its powerful Chaos Emeralds to achieve his goal. Sonic and his friends catch wind of Eggman's plans and set out to stop him. Along the way, they collect rings and power-ups to aid them in their quest. As Sonic and his friends get closer to Eggman's base, they face increasingly challenging obstacles and adversaries. Eventually, they confront Dr. Eggman in a final showdown aboard the Egg Utopia. With teamwork and determination, Sonic and his friends defeat Eggman once again, saving the world from his evil plans.
