Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Shadow the hedgehog

Story:

Uncover the story of Shadow, who is on a quest to regain his memory and understand his true identity. The narrative unfolds amidst an alien invasion led by the Black Arms, against which Shadow must defend the Earth. Throughout the game, Shadow encounters various characters, including Dr. Robotnik, previously known as Dr. Eggman, who is entangled in the conflict with his own agenda. The game's progression and ending depend on the player's choices, leading to multiple possible outcomes.
