Latest News

The upcoming release of "Sonic X Shadow Generations" is generating excitement among fans of the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise. This new game promises a thrilling adventure where players can experience the best of both worlds: the high-speed action of Sonic and the intense narrative of Shadow. With a remastered version of the classic "Sonic Generations" included, players will be able to enjoy iconic stages with upgraded visuals and gameplay. Additionally, an all-new story campaign featuring Shadow the Hedgehog will offer a deep dive into his character, complete with new powers and abilities. As the game is set to launch in Autumn 2024, anticipation is building for what is expected to be a standout addition to the Sonic series.
