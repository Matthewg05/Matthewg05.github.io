Sonic Lost World

Story:

The adventure begins with Sonic and his loyal friend Tails chasing their long-time nemesis through the skies. Dr. Robotnik, in his relentless pursuit of world domination, has harnessed the power of the six menacing creatures known as the Deadly Six. However, his control over these dangerous beings falters, leading to an unpredictable uprising.
