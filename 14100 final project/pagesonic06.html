Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic the Hedgehog (2006)

Story:

Dr. Robotnik discovers that Princess Elise holds a secret, the "Flames of Disaster," which he seeks to exploit for his own nefarious purposes. Dr. Robotnik's attempts to kidnap the princess are thwarted by Sonic, who steps in to rescue her. Meanwhile, a new character, Silver the Hedgehog, arrives from the future on a mission to prevent the catastrophic events he has witnessed in his time. He believes Sonic to be the "Iblis Trigger," the one responsible for his world's dire future.
