Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Colors

Story:

Sonic and Tails investigate the suspicious Egg Planet Park. This amusement park, created by Dr. Eggman, is supposedly a gesture of goodwill after his past misdeeds. However, Sonic and Tails soon discover that the park is a facade for Eggman's true plan: to harness the energy of the Wisps, an alien race with unique powers.
