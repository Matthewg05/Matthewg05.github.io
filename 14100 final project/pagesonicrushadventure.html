Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Rush Adventure

Story:

Sonic and Tails find themselves transported to an alternate dimension known as the Sol Dimension. Upon arrival, they encounter a pirate named Captain Whisker, who serves under the command of the evil Dr. Eggman and his counterpart from the Sol Dimension, Dr. Eggman Nega. The story revolves around Sonic and Blaze teaming up with the inhabitants of the Sol Dimension, including Marine the Raccoon, to collect the Chaos Emeralds and repair the damaged energy core of the dimension's main island.
