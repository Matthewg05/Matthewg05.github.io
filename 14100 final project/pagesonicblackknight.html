Sonic and the Black Knight

Story:

is transported into the world of Arthurian legend, where he must embark on a quest to defeat the evil wizard Merlina and the corrupted King Arthur. In the game, Sonic wields the sacred sword Caliburn and is bestowed the title of "Sir Sonic the Hedgehog." He encounters various characters from Arthurian legend, including King Arthur himself, as well as Sir Lancelot (who is later revealed to be Shadow the Hedgehog), Sir Gawain (who is actually Knuckles the Echidna), and Sir Percival (who is actually Blaze the Cat).
