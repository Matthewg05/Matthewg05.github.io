Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic CD

Story:

travels to Little Planet, a world that appears once every year, thanks to the power of the Time Stones. Dr. Robotnik, also known as Eggman, seeks to harness this power to control time itself and conquer the universe. Sonic's goal is to save Little Planet and its Time Stones from Dr. Robotnik's clutches. Along the way, Sonic encounters his arch-nemesis, Metal Sonic, who serves as Robotnik's enforcer.
