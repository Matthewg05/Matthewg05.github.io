Sonic the Hedgehog Fan Encyclopedia

Sonic Advance

Story:

Dr. Eggman discovers a way to harness the power of the Chaos Emeralds to fuel his latest diabolical scheme. He plans to use the Emeralds to power his latest invention, the Egg Rocket, and take over the world. Sonic and his friends catch wind of Eggman's plans and set out to thwart him.
