Sonic Mania

Story:

Sonic, Tails, and Knuckles chase after Dr. Eggman as he seeks to harness the power of the mysterious Phantom Ruby.Throughout the game, Sonic and his allies travel through various zones, each with its own unique themes and challenges, reminiscent of classic Sonic games. Along the way, they encounter familiar faces, including classic bosses like Metal Sonic and new adversaries empowered by the Phantom Ruby.As Sonic and his friends progress, they uncover Dr. Eggman's plan to use the Phantom Ruby to manipulate reality itself, creating distorted versions of familiar zones and challenging the heroes in unexpected ways. Ultimately, Sonic and his allies must confront Dr. Eggman and thwart his plans to save their world from chaos.
