Sonic The Hedgehog 2

Story:

In the thrilling sequel to his first adventure, Sonic the Hedgehog teams up with his new friend, Miles Prower to thwart the nefarious plans of Dr. Ivo Robotnik. This time, Dr. Robotnik is after the powerful Chaos Emeralds, and it's up to Sonic and Miles to stop him from unleashing chaos upon the world. With high-speed chases and epic battles, Sonic the Hedgehog 2 takes the excitement to new heights as our heroes dash towards a climactic showdown to save the day.
