Sonic Adventure 2

Story:

Dr. Robotnik unveils his grand plan to conquer the world through the power of the Chaos Emeralds, with a new mysterious ally. Meanwhile, Sonic and his loyal friend Miles Prower, often called Tails, race against time to thwart Robotnik's schemes. Alongside allies like Knuckles and Amy, they embark on a high-speed journey to collect the Emeralds before Robotnik can use them to fuel his ultimate newly recoverd family weapon, the Space Colony ARK.
