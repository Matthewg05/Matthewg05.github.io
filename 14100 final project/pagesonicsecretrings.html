Sonic and the Secret Rings

Story:

Sonic is transported into the world of the Arabian Nights after opening a mystical book called the "Arabian Nights." In this world, Sonic meets Shahra the Genie, who explains that the evil Erazor Djinn is threatening to destroy the world by erasing it from the book. To stop Erazor Djinn and save the Arabian Nights world, Sonic must embark on a quest to collect the seven World Rings, powerful artifacts that can grant wishes. Along the way, Sonic encounters various characters from Arabian Nights folklore.
